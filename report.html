Board Insights
📈
Long-term Growth
Income has grown from £647k in 2008–09 to £4.22M in 2025–26 — a 6.5× increase over 17 years.
💰
Profit Trajectory
Net profit peaked at £636k in 2022–23. Post-pandemic average £565k per annum.
🏠
Revenue Diversification
Life insurance, client fees and conveyancing now represent over 50% of total income.
⚖️
Commission Efficiency
Commission paid as a % of income has remained stable at 66–68% over time.
📊
Margin Compression
Net margins compressed from 25% (2010–11) to 13–16% recently. Overhead management is key.
🎯
2025–26 Performance
Record income of £4.22M and net profit of £574k (13.6% margin). Q2 strongest quarter at £1.115M.
